CARB Compliance Management

California's Air Resources Board has strict emissions requirements that apply to any fleet operating in the state. We track your compliance status, manage deadlines, and handle CARB portal submissions so you avoid fines and stay road-legal in California.

What's Included:

  • Fleet compliance status tracking
  • Deadline monitoring and alerts
  • CARB portal submissions
  • Compliance documentation and reporting

IRP Registration & Renewals

The International Registration Plan governs apportioned registration for vehicles operating across multiple jurisdictions. We handle new registrations, renewals, and fleet additions so your trucks stay legal wherever they run.

What's Included:

  • New IRP account setup
  • Annual renewals
  • Adding or removing vehicles
  • Weight group changes and amendments

UCR Registration

The Unified Carrier Registration requires annual registration for motor carriers, brokers, and freight forwarders operating in interstate commerce. We make sure you're registered and compliant each year.

What's Included:

  • Annual UCR registration
  • Fee calculation based on fleet size
  • Filing and confirmation

Weight Distance Tax Filings

Several states impose weight distance or mileage-based taxes on commercial vehicles operating within their borders. These require separate filings beyond IFTA. We handle reporting for all applicable states.

States Covered:

  • New York (HUT)
  • Kentucky (KYU)
  • New Mexico (Weight Distance Tax)
  • Oregon (Weight-Mile Tax)
  • Connecticut (Mileage Tax)

What's Included:

  • Mileage tracking by state
  • Tax calculation and return preparation
  • Filing and payment coordination
  • Manage ongoing reporting requirements
